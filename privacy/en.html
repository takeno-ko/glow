🌸 Glow Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-03

This Policy describes how user information is handled in the mobile application Glow ("the App"), provided by the developer takenoko ("we", "us").

1. Information We Collect

The App stores data entered by the user (pimple logs, skin condition, daily logs of care/meals/sleep/period, nickname, character preferences, friend codes, etc.) only on the user's device. This data is not sent to our servers.

2. Personally Identifiable Information

The App does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, address, phone number, email address, location, or contacts. The nickname is optional and can be set, changed, or removed freely.

3. Anonymous Usage Analytics

To improve the App, anonymous usage data may be sent to PostHog (postHog.com; data stored in US/EU regions). The data sent includes:

  • Anonymous events such as screen views and taps
  • OS type, OS version, app version
  • A randomly generated anonymous identifier

No personally identifiable information or the content of recorded skin data is sent.

4. Advertising (Free Tier Only)

The free tier of the App shows advertisements via Google AdMob. AdMob may send the following types of information to Google for the purpose of ad delivery:

  • Advertising ID (Android Advertising ID / iOS IDFA)
  • OS type, OS version, device type
  • Approximate location (country / region)

The App itself does not pass user-recorded skin logs to advertisers. For details on Google's ad delivery, see Google's advertising policies. Subscribing to Supporter (monthly) removes all ads.

5. Notifications

Daily reminders operate as local notifications on the device, only when the user enables them in settings. Notification content is not routed through our servers.

6. In-App Purchases (Supporter)

Payments for the Supporter (monthly) plan are processed directly by Google Play / the App Store. We do not receive or store payment information such as credit card numbers.

7. Sharing

When the user explicitly taps a share button to send images or text to social media or other apps, that content is handled by the receiving service, and that service's privacy policy applies.

8. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob (advertising delivery, free tier only)
  • PostHog (anonymous usage analytics)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play (distribution and billing)
  • Instagram and other services the user explicitly chooses as a share target

9. Data Retention and Deletion

All logged data in the App is stored on the user's device. Uninstalling the App removes this data from the device. From "Backup data" in settings, users can export their data as a JSON file at any time. The advertising ID can be reset or opted out from the device settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads; iOS: Settings → Privacy → Advertising).

10. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Users under 13 should use the App with parental consent.

11. Changes to This Policy

This Policy may be updated without prior notice. When updated, the latest date and content will be posted on this page.
